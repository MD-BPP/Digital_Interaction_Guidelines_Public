Fundamentals Level

The fundamentals level consists of nine compulsory papers split across two modules; The Knowledge Module (F1-F3) and the Skills Module (F4-F9). Depending on your previous education you may qualify for exemptions from some of the papers at this level

Our full-time fundamentals level programmes give you exemptions from these exams. Our part-time courses prepare you to sit these papers with the ACCA.

Practical Experience Requirement (PER)

To complete PER you must complete 36 months' employment in an accounting or finance-related role. During this time you will need to record and report on your progress and acheive a number of performance objectives.

The three years can consist of full-time or part-time work, temporary positions and internship as long as they meet the ACCA requirements.
