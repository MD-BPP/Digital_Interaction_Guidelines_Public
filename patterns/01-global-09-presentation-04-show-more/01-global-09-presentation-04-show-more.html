Assistant Accountant Apprenticeship Standard Level 3

An assistant accountant provides support to internal and external customers and will work within a practice or the finance function of an organisation. Part of their role will involve assisting in the day-to-day financial activities such as data entry to month-end management accounts and/or year-end financial statements.
